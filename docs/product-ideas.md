# MeetMinds Product Ideas

This document captures ideas and concepts for future development of the MeetMinds platform.

## App Integrations via MCP Servers

### Integration with Popular Productivity Tools

- Gmail integration for reading, deleting, and responding to emails through agent conversations
- WhatsApp integration for messaging capabilities
- Google Calendar integration for scheduling and event management
- Google Docs integration for document creation and editing
- Notion integration for workspace management and content creation
- Microsoft Outlook integration for email and calendar functions
- Excel integration for data analysis and spreadsheet management
- Word integration for document creation and editing
- PowerPoint integration for presentation creation and editing

These integrations would allow users to ask their agents to perform tasks like:

- "Read my unread emails and summarize important ones"
- "Schedule a meeting with John for next Tuesday"
- "Create a document with the notes from our last conversation"
- "Prepare a presentation about our quarterly results"

## Pricing Plans and Tiers

### Free Tier

- 2 agents
- 2 meetings per month
- Local download of meeting recordings
- Up to 2GB cloud storage
- Up to 60 minutes of meeting recordings
- Default Stream storage (up to 2 weeks)
- Basic agent capabilities

### Plus Tier

Everything in free plus:

- 5 agents
- 5 meetings per month
- Local download of meeting recordings
- Up to 10GB cloud storage
- Up to 300 minutes of meeting recordings
- Monthly: $15/month
- Annual: $149/year (equivalent to $12.42/month)

### Premium Tier

Everything in Plus plus:

- 15 agents
- 15 meetings per month
- Up to 50GB cloud storage
- Up to 900 minutes of meeting recordings
- Monthly: $25/month
- Annual: $249/year (equivalent to $20.75/month)

### Pro Tier

Everything in Premium plus:

- Unlimited agents
- Unlimited meetings per month
- Unlimited cloud storage
- Unlimited meeting recordings
- Monthly: $100/month
- Annual: $999/year (equivalent to $83.25/month)

**Note:** Unlimited use is subject to abuse guardrails

### Value Propositions

- Annual subscriptions offer 17-25% savings compared to monthly plans
- Clear progression from basic to advanced usage
- Competitive pricing for individual users and small teams
- Scalable options for growing businesses
